# 🚀 Indie Affiliate Site Playbook

> **From idea to revenue in 90 days.** A complete, validation-first framework for solo indie builders to launch profitable affiliate niche sites using AI-native workflows.

[![PRs Welcome](https://img.shields.io/badge/PRs-welcome-brightgreen.svg)](CONTRIBUTING.md)
[![Community](https://img.shields.io/badge/community-open-blue.svg)](https://github.com/David-DL-Space/indie-affiliate-site-playbook/discussions)

**Universal framework** — applies to any tool/content site: testing tools, calculators, generators, converters, comparison sites, resource hubs.

---

## 🎯 What You'll Build

- **3-6 core features** — interactive tools, calculators, or resource directories
- **20-50 SEO pages** — buying guides, how-tos, comparisons
- **Affiliate monetization** — Amazon Associates, Impact.com, or direct programs
- **10-50 backlinks** — from GitHub awesome-lists + niche blogs
- **$100-500/mo in 6 months** (conservative), $500-2k/mo (aggressive)

**Investment:** 2-3 weeks full-time OR 1-2 months part-time, <$50 cost (domain only)

## 📖 The 8 Stages

| Stage | What | Time | Key Metric |
|---|---|---|---|
| **0. Validation** | Find proof someone is making >$500/mo | 48h | **HARD GATE** |
| **1. Tech Stack** | Choose framework + boilerplate | 1 day | - |
| **2. MVP Build** | 1 core feature + 2 auxiliary | 3-5 days | Lighthouse >90 |
| **3. SEO Foundation** | Sitemap, schema, GSC | 2 days | >5 pages indexed |
| **4. Content Strategy** | Long-tail keywords → blog posts | Ongoing | 20 posts by Month 2 |
| **5. Affiliate Integration** | Product cards + event tracking | 2 days | CTR >0.5% |
| **6. Analytics** | GA4 + GSC pipeline | 1 day | Dashboard live |
| **7. Backlinks** | Awesome-lists + niche blogs | 3-6 mo | 10+ by Month 3 |
| **8. Scale** | A/B test, expand | Ongoing | $500/mo by Month 6 |

---

## 🔥 Core Principles

1. **48h validation gate** — Don't build until you find proof of revenue
2. **Long-tail SEO** — 50 keywords @ 100/mo > 1 keyword @ 10k ranked #10
3. **AI-assisted, human-reviewed** — Use AI for speed, add human quality
4. **Backlinks > content** — 20 posts + 10 links > 100 posts + 0 links
5. **Data-driven pivots** — Clear abandon/double-down signals each month

## 🛠️ Recommended Tech Stack

**Frontend:** Next.js 15 / Astro / Nuxt  
**Styling:** Tailwind CSS  
**Deploy:** Vercel / Cloudflare Pages / Netlify (all free)  
**Analytics:** GA4 + Google Search Console  
**Affiliate:** Amazon Associates / Impact.com / ShareASale  

**Cost:** $0-10/mo (domain $10/yr is your only必需 expense)

**Framework-agnostic** — Use what you know. Core playbook (validation, SEO, backlinks) works with any stack.

## 🎓 Decision Trees

### Month 1: Launch or Pivot?
- ✅ **GSC >10 impr/day + 1+ backlink** → Continue
- ❌ **3+ metrics below** → Pivot or abandon

### Month 2: Growth or Stall?
- ✅ **Impr >50/day + 1 keyword in page 2** → Double down
- ❌ **<20/day after 20 posts + 5 links** → Niche too small, pivot

### Month 3: Revenue or Rethink?
- ✅ **First order (even $0.50)** → PMF validated, scale
- ❌ **<30 sessions/day, flat** → SEO ceiling, pivot

### Month 6: Scale or Sell?
- ✅ **>$500/mo** → Scale to $2k OR sell (24-36x profit)
- ❌ **<$100/mo, 6 mo flat** → Sell $1-3k, next idea

Full matrices in [`references/full-playbook.md`](references/full-playbook.md) → "When to Pivot"
