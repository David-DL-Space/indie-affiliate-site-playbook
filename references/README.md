## What Is This?

A **tactical playbook** (not theory, not inspiration) for building affiliate niche sites as a one-person company. Covers:

- ✅ **Idea validation** — 48-hour gate to avoid wasting weeks on bad ideas
- ✅ **Tech stack selection** — optimized for solo builders (Next.js/Vercel/Tailwind recommended, but framework-agnostic)
- ✅ **MVP build** — ship in 1-2 weeks, not months
- ✅ **SEO foundation** — sitemap, schema, GSC setup from day 1
- ✅ **Long-tail keyword strategy** — 50 keywords @ 100 searches/mo > 1 keyword @ 10k ranked #10
- ✅ **Affiliate integration** — product cards, event tracking, conversion optimization
- ✅ **Backlink acquisition** — GitHub awesome-lists + niche blog outreach (20-40% conversion)
- ✅ **Data-driven pivots** — clear "abandon signals" at months 1, 2, 3, 6

**Framework applies to any tool/content niche:**
- Testing tools (audio test, color blindness, typing speed, wifi analyzer)
- Calculators (mortgage, BMI, pregnancy, retirement, crypto ROI)
- Generators (QR code, password, invoice, resume, color palette)
- Converters (unit, file format, timezone, currency)
- Comparison sites (best X for Y, X vs Y, tool directories)
- Resource hubs (free fonts, stock photos, code snippets)

**Realistic investment:**

- Skills: Can code (web dev), understand basic SEO, have AI tooling

### 3. Start Your Own Site

**Stage 0 (48h validation):**
1. Pick a niche idea
2. Find 3 competitors
3. Estimate their traffic (SimilarWeb, Ahrefs free tier)
4. Verify at least 1 is making >$500/mo (look for affiliate links, check Privacy Policy)
5. **Decision gate:** If you can't find proof of revenue, pivot to a different niche

**Stages 1-3 (1-2 weeks):**
1. Choose tech stack (use recommended Next.js + Vercel, or your preferred stack)
2. Build MVP (1 core feature + 2 auxiliary)
3. Deploy + GSC setup
4. Submit sitemap

**Stages 4-8 (ongoing):**
Follow the playbook month by month. Use decision trees at months 1, 2, 3, 6 to decide: pivot, persevere, or scale.

## Decision Trees (When to Pivot)

### Month 1 Gate
- ✅ GSC impressions >10/day + 1+ backlink merged → **Continue**
- ⚠️ 1-2 metrics below → **Diagnose + wait 2 weeks**
- ❌ 3+ metrics below → **Pivot or abandon**

### Month 2 Gate
- ✅ Impressions >50/day + 1 keyword in page 2 → **Double down**
- ⚠️ Impressions <20/day → **Add 10-20 blog posts**
- ❌ Still <20/day after 20 posts + 5 backlinks → **Niche too small, pivot**

### Month 3 Gate
- ✅ First order (even $0.50) → **PMF validated, scale**
- ⚠️ 0 orders but clicks >20 → **Wait 2 weeks (conversion delay)**
- ❌ Sessions <30/day, flat trend → **SEO ceiling, pivot to paid or abandon**

### Month 6 Gate
- ✅ Revenue >$500/mo → **Scale to $2k OR sell (24-36x profit)**
- ⚠️ Revenue $100-200/mo → **Maintain OR start 2nd site**
- ❌ Revenue <$100/mo, 6 months flat → **Sell $1-3k, next idea**

Full decision matrices + diagnostic flowcharts in `full-playbook.md`.

## Key Principles

1. **48h validation gate** — Don't waste 2 weeks building something nobody wants
2. **Long-tail SEO > big keywords** — 50 keywords @ 100/mo = 5000/mo aggregated volume
3. **AI-assisted, human-reviewed** — Use AI for 80% speed, add 20% human touch to avoid penalties
4. **Backlinks > content** — 20 posts + 10 backlinks > 100 posts + 0 backlinks
5. **Data-driven pivots** — Ego kills indie projects. Follow the data, not your gut.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to use Next.js + Vercel?**  
A: No. The playbook is framework-agnostic. Next.js + Vercel is the **recommended** stack for solo builders (fastest MVP, free hosting, SEO-friendly), but you can use Nuxt/Astro/SvelteKit + Cloudflare/Netlify and the framework still applies.

**Q: What if I'm not technical?**  
A: This playbook assumes you can code. If you're non-technical, consider using no-code tools (Webflow, Framer, WordPress) and skip stages 1-2. Stages 0, 4-8 (validation, content, backlinks) are still 100% applicable.

**Q: Is this legal? Ethical?**  
A: Yes and yes. Affiliate marketing is legal worldwide. Amazon Associates, Impact.com, ShareASale are all legitimate programs. You're helping users find products they need and earning a commission. The playbook emphasizes quality content (not spam) and transparency (disclose affiliate links).

**Q: What about AI content penalties?**  
A: Google penalizes AI spam (low-quality, auto-generated, no human review). This playbook uses AI for **speed** (SERP analysis, outlines, drafts) but requires **human review** (fact-check, add personal insights, remove AI tone via `humanizer` skill). Quality > quantity.

**Q: How much can I realistically make?**  
A: Conservative: $100-500/mo in 6 months. Aggressive: $500-2k/mo in 6 months if you hit all milestones. Outlier: $5-10k/mo in 12 months if you scale to 3-5 niche sites. Don't expect to quit your job in month 3.

**Q: What if my niche doesn't have Amazon products?**  
A: Use other affiliate networks:
- **Impact.com / ShareASale** — thousands of merchants (hosting, SaaS, ecommerce)
- **Direct programs** — e.g., Bluehost (web hosting, 50% commission), Shopify (affiliate program), Elementor (WordPress)
- **Display ads** — Mediavine, AdThrive (need >50k sessions/mo to qualify)

**Q: Can I use this for B2B SaaS?**  
A: Partially. Validation (stage 0), SEO (stages 3-4), backlinks (stage 7) apply. Affiliate integration (stage 5) changes — B2B SaaS typically has direct partner programs with higher commissions ($100-500 per signup vs. $2-5 per Amazon order).

**Q: How do I know when to pivot?**  
A: Follow the decision trees. If you hit Month 1 gate (GSC <10 impr/day, 0 backlinks, 3+ metrics below threshold), pivot. Don't waste 3 months on a dead niche. See "When to Pivot" section in `SKILL.md`.

**Q: What if awesome-list PRs get rejected?**  
A: Normal. Awesome-list merge rates vary 10-80% depending on list quality and your submission. If rejected:
1. Read rejection reason (usually: "not notable enough" or "doesn't fit category")
2. Try 3-5 other lists
3. If 5 rejections → your site might not be ready (need more features, better UI, or social proof)

Focus on niche blog outreach (Tier 1) — that's where high-value backlinks come from.
